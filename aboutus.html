    About Kahok-Dianhok Beach Resort

    Welcome to Kahok-Dianhok Beach Resort, your serene sanctuary nestled along the stunning shores of Tagum City in the Philippines. Established on August 20, 2021, in the picturesque setting of Purok 1, Abucado, Tagnanan, Mapising, Mabini, Davao De Oro 8807, our resort is dedicated to providing guests with a unique blend of luxury, affordability, and natural beauty.


    Our Mission and Vision

    At Kahok-Dianhok Beach Resort, our mission is to create unforgettable experiences for our guests by offering exceptional hospitality and personalized service. We envision our resort as a haven where guests can escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and immerse themselves in the tranquility of nature.

    Location and Setting

    Situated amidst lush landscapes and breathtaking ocean views, Kahok-Dianhok Beach Resort offers an idyllic setting for relaxation and adventure. Our resort is conveniently located in Purok 1, Abucado, Tagnanan, Mapising, Mabini, Davao De Oro 8807, providing easy access to nearby attractions and the pristine beaches of Tagum City.

    Accommodations

    Experience comfort in our range of accommodations, including cozy nipa huts and spacious beachfront kubos. Each of our accommodations is thoughtfully designed to provide a comfortable and memorable stay for every guest.
